'Personal Information' is information or an opinion that identifies you as an individual. We do not collect nor store any personal information.

Sensitive Information

Sensitive information is defined in the US Act to include information or opinion about such things as an individual's racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, membership of a political association, religious or philosophical beliefs, membership of a professional body, criminal records or health information. We will not attempt to obtain nor record any sensitive information.
